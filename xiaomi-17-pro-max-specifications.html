Xiaomi 17 Pro Max

October 24, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Titanium Black

Overview

The Xiaomi 17 Pro Max stands as Xiaomi's most ambitious flagship smartphone launched in September 2025, representing the pinnacle of Android innovation with its groundbreaking dual-screen design. Featuring a massive 6.9-inch main display and a unique 2.9-inch rear AMOLED screen for notifications and selfies, this device redefines premium smartphone experiences. Powered by the cutting-edge Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 processor and equipped with a colossal 7,500 mAh silicon carbon battery, it delivers unmatched performance and endurance.

With its Leica-engineered triple 50MP camera system featuring a 5x periscope telephoto lens, 3500 nits peak brightness display, and advanced HyperOS 3 with comprehensive AI capabilities, the Xiaomi 17 Pro Max excels in photography, visual clarity, and intelligent features. Priced from approximately $825-$1,063 USD depending on configuration, it offers exceptional flagship specifications with competitive value, challenging premium devices like the iPhone 17 Pro Max while maintaining superior battery capacity and charging speeds.
