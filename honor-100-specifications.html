Overview

The Honor 100 is a modern Android smartphone featuring the Snapdragon 7 Gen 3 chipset and a large 6.7-inch OLED 120Hz display.

It was released in November 2023, boasting a powerful dual-camera setup with a 50MP main sensor and 100W fast charging for its 5000mAh battery.
