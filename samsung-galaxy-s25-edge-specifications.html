Overview

The Samsung Galaxy S25 Edge is a conceptual flagship device, blending premium design with next-generation performance and a focus on on-device AI capabilities.\

It's designed to be a balanced alternative to the Ultra model, offering a superb camera, potent processor, and a dynamic AMOLED display in a sleek form factor.\
