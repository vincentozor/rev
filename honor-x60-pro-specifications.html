Overview

The Honor X60 Pro is a mid-range smartphone known for its large battery, curved AMOLED display, and 108MP primary camera.

It runs on the Qualcomm Snapdragon 6 Gen 1 chipset with MagicOS 8 based on Android 14. Key features include a massive 6600mAh battery with 66W charging, and some variants support two-way Beidou satellite SMS.
