Overview

The Honor 100 Pro is a premium smartphone featuring a powerful Snapdragon 8 Gen 2 processor, a stunning OLED display, and a versatile triple camera system with a 50MP main sensor.

It was announced in November 2023, offering high-end performance and a sleek design.
