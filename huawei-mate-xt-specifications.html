Huawei Mate XT

October 25, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Mystic Black

Overview

The Huawei Mate XT 5G is a groundbreaking tri-fold smartphone that seamlessly transforms from a compact phone into a large immersive display experience, designed for power users and multitaskers.

Featuring a triple-screen flexible OLED design, top-tier camera system, large battery with fast charging, and a premium build, it sets a new benchmark in mobile technology.
