Samsung Galaxy Z Flip 7 vs Motorola Razr Ultra (2025)

October 27, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Graphite

Overview

The Samsung Galaxy Z Flip 7 and Motorola Razr Ultra (2025) represent the pinnacle of flip phone technology in 2025, each taking a different approach to clamshell foldable excellence. The Galaxy Z Flip 7 emphasizes Samsung's ecosystem integration, refined design, and seven years of software support, while the Razr Ultra focuses on raw power with flagship-grade specifications and the fastest charging in its class.

Both devices feature expansive cover screens that minimize the need to unfold, powerful processors, and improved durability. The Z Flip 7 starts at $1,099 with Samsung's Exynos 2500 chip, while the Razr Ultra commands $1,299 but delivers Qualcomm's Snapdragon 8 Elite and double the storage. This comparison will help you determine which premium flip phone best suits your needs.

Final Verdict

Choose Samsung Galaxy Z Flip 7 if:

  • You value long-term software support with 7 years of updates
  • You're invested in the Samsung ecosystem (Galaxy Watch, Buds, SmartThings)
  • You want Samsung DeX for desktop productivity
  • You prefer a slightly lighter and thinner design at 188g
  • Better resale value and Samsung's established repair network matter to you

Choose Motorola Razr Ultra (2025) if:

  • You want the absolute best performance with Snapdragon 8 Elite
  • You need more storage (512GB base, up to 1TB)
  • Fast charging is a priority - 68W vs Samsung's 25W
  • You prefer a larger battery (4700mAh vs 4300mAh)
  • You want premium finishes like Alcantara or wood-inspired materials
  • More flexible cover screen app support appeals to you

Bottom Line: The Samsung Galaxy Z Flip 7 is the smart choice for ecosystem integration and long-term value with its industry-leading update commitment, while the Motorola Razr Ultra (2025) delivers superior raw performance, storage capacity, and charging speeds for power users willing to pay a premium. Both are exceptional flip phones that prioritize different strengths.
