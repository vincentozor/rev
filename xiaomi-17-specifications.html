Xiaomi 17

October 24, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Midnight Black

Overview

The Xiaomi 17 represents a revolutionary leap in smartphone technology, launched in September 2025 as part of Xiaomi's most ambitious flagship series. Powered by the world's first Snapdragon 8 Elite Gen 5 chipset and featuring an unprecedented 7,000 mAh silicon carbon battery, this device sets new standards for performance and endurance in the premium smartphone market.

With its Leica-tuned triple camera system featuring 50MP sensors across all lenses, 6.3-inch 1.5K LTPO OLED display with 3500 nits peak brightness, and advanced HyperOS 3 with AI capabilities, the Xiaomi 17 delivers exceptional photography, stunning visuals, and intelligent features. At a starting price of $748 USD, it offers flagship performance with remarkable value, selling over a million units in China during its first week.
