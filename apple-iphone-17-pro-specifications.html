Apple iPhone 17 Pro

October 26, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro

Overview

The Apple iPhone 17 Pro is expected to be Apple's top-tier flagship model for 2025, rumored to feature a major redesign and a groundbreaking camera system. Key rumored upgrades include a new under-display camera (UDC) for a notch/Dynamic Island-free screen, a more powerful A19 Pro Bionic chip, and an advanced periscope zoom lens.

It will likely run on the AI-focused iOS 19 and boast an ultra-bright, edge-to-edge LTPO ProMotion display, a durable Titanium chassis, and new, power-efficient battery technology to deliver peak performance and extended endurance.
