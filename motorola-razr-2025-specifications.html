Overview

The Motorola Razr 2025 is a next-generation foldable smartphone designed to deliver premium performance, stunning visuals, and an ultra-portable form factor. It continues the Razr legacy with modern hardware and advanced AI features.

With its immersive display, flagship processor, versatile camera system, and all-day battery life, the Motorola Razr 2025 is built for users who want style, power, and cutting-edge technology in a compact foldable design.
