Samsung Galaxy S25 Full Phone Specification

Published: January 15, 2025
By: Tech Review Team
Read time: 10 min
Cosmic Black

Overview

The Samsung Galaxy S25 represents the latest flagship smartphone from Samsung, launched in February 2025. It features a compact 6.2-inch design with cutting-edge performance powered by the Snapdragon 8 Elite for Galaxy chipset.

This device brings significant improvements in AI capabilities, performance efficiency, and display technology while maintaining Samsung's premium build quality with Gorilla Glass Victus 2 protection and IP68 water resistance.

Design & Display

Display Size 6.2 inches, 94.4 cm² (~91.1% screen-to-body ratio)
Display Type Dynamic LTPO AMOLED 2X, HDR10+
Resolution 1080 x 2340 pixels, 19.5:9 ratio (~416 ppi density)
Refresh Rate 120Hz adaptive refresh rate
Brightness 2600 nits peak brightness
Protection Corning Gorilla Glass Victus 2
Dimensions 146.9 x 70.5 x 7.2 mm (5.78 x 2.78 x 0.28 in)
Weight 162 g (5.71 oz)
Build Glass front and back (Gorilla Glass Victus 2), Armor Aluminum 2 frame
IP Rating IP68 dust/water resistant (up to 1.5m for 30 min)

Performance

Processor Qualcomm Snapdragon 8 Elite for Galaxy (3nm)
CPU Octa-core (2x3.53 GHz Oryon V2 Phoenix L + 6x3.06 GHz Oryon V2 Phoenix M)
GPU Adreno 830
RAM 12GB LPDDR5X
Storage 128GB, 256GB, 512GB UFS 4.0 (non-expandable)
Cooling System Enhanced vapor chamber cooling (15% larger than S24)
OS Android 15, One UI 7 (7 years of OS and security updates)

Camera System

Main Camera 50 MP, f/1.8, 24mm (wide), 1/1.56", 1.0µm, Dual Pixel PDAF, OIS
Ultra Telephoto N/A (Standard model does not include ultra telephoto)
Wide Telephoto 10 MP, f/2.4, 67mm (telephoto), 1/3.94", 1.0µm, PDAF, OIS, 3x optical zoom
Ultrawide Camera 12 MP, f/2.2, 13mm, 120˚ (ultrawide), 1/2.55", 1.4µm
Front Camera 12 MP, f/2.2, 26mm (wide), Dual Pixel PDAF
Video Recording 8K@24/30fps, 4K@30/60fps, 1080p@30/60/240fps, HDR10+, gyro-EIS
AI Features AI-powered scene optimization, Night mode, Portrait mode, Pro mode, Super Steady video

Battery & Connectivity

Battery Capacity 4000 mAh (non-removable)
Charging 25W wired charging, 15W wireless charging, 4.5W reverse wireless charging
Battery Life Up to 29 hours talk time, optimized with AI battery management
5G Bands 5G SA/NSA/Sub6/mmWave (region dependent)
Wi-Fi Wi-Fi 7 (802.11be), tri-band, Wi-Fi Direct
Bluetooth Bluetooth 6.0, A2DP, LE, aptX HD
NFC Yes (Samsung Pay certified)
USB USB Type-C 3.2, OTG support
Other Features Ultra-Wideband (UWB), Dual SIM (Nano-SIM + eSIM), Always-on display, Ultrasonic fingerprint sensor

AI & Software Features

AI Assistant Galaxy AI with enhanced voice commands and contextual awareness
NPU Integrated Hexagon NPU in Snapdragon 8 Elite with 45 TOPS performance
Real-time Translation Live Translate for calls and messages in 13+ languages
Camera AI AI photo editing, object removal, generative fill, enhanced night photography
Battery AI Adaptive battery optimization with usage pattern learning
Security Samsung Knox Vault, ultrasonic fingerprint sensor, face recognition
Software Updates 7 years of major Android OS updates and security patches (until 2032)

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Snapdragon 8 Elite offers exceptional performance with 40% better benchmark scores
  • Compact and lightweight design at only 162g
  • Bright 2600 nits AMOLED display with 120Hz refresh rate
  • 7 years of software updates guaranteed
  • Advanced Galaxy AI features with real-time translation
  • Premium build quality with Gorilla Glass Victus 2 and IP68 rating

Cons

  • Only 25W wired charging (slower than competitors)
  • 4000mAh battery is smaller than S25+ and Ultra
  • No microSD card slot for storage expansion
  • Camera hardware unchanged from S24
