How to Choose the Right iPhone 17 Case

Key Considerations:

  • Drop Protection: Most cases offer between 6-15 feet of drop protection. Consider your lifestyle and accident history when choosing protection level
  • MagSafe Compatibility: Essential for wireless charging and accessories. Ensure your case has proper magnetic alignment
  • Camera Control Implementation: Look for cases with proper covers rather than cutouts for full functionality
  • Material Quality: TPU, polycarbonate, and vegan leather offer different balances of protection and feel
  • Price vs. Value: The $35-$50 range typically offers the best balance of protection, features, and durability
