iQOO Neo 11 Launches Today with Snapdragon 8 Elite and 7500mAh Battery

October 28, 2025
PhoneSpecs Pro

SHANGHAI, OCTOBER 30, 2025 - iQOO has officially launched its latest gaming powerhouse, the iQOO Neo 11, today at a special event. The device features the groundbreaking Snapdragon 8 Elite processor and a massive 7500mAh battery, setting new standards for performance smartphones in 2025.

The launch event showcased what iQOO calls the "strongest Neo series phone to date," with the Neo 11 being the only model in its class to combine a 2K resolution screen with a 7500mAh超大容量电池, positioning it as the new benchmark for mid-range performance flagships.

"iQOO Neo 11 will be the first choice in the mid-range performance flagship market," said iQOO Product Manager Luo Jiahui. "With the Snapdragon 8 Elite and our proprietary cooling technology, we're delivering flagship-level performance at an accessible price point."

Gaming & Performance Features

The iQOO Neo 11 comes packed with gaming-specific technologies that set it apart from competitors:

Monster Engine

Same as iQOO 15's Monster engine, supporting native 144FPS for MOBA games and extreme graphics mode.

Advanced Cooling

Industry-leading 8K VC liquid cooling system, one of the largest in the industry for sustained performance.

Campus Gaming Network

Dedicated campus gaming network optimization for stable connections in challenging environments.

2K+144FPS Concurrency

One of the few phones in its price range supporting 2K resolution and 144FPS super resolution and frame rate concurrent display.

Design & Build Quality

Metal Frame

Upgraded to aviation aluminum metal middle frame with AG frosted glass back, a significant improvement over previous plastic frames.

3D Ultrasonic Fingerprint

Advanced 3D ultrasonic fingerprint recognition for faster, more accurate unlocking even with wet hands.

IP68 & IP69 Rating

Dual IP68 and IP69 dust and water resistance ratings, providing comprehensive protection.

Software & System Features

The iQOO Neo 11 will be among the first devices to run the new OriginOS 6 based on Android 16. The system brings improved fluidity and smart features, with the product manager emphasizing "the strongest Neo + the smoothest OriginOS" combination.

Additional notable features include symmetric stereo dual speakers, improved X-axis linear motor, and comprehensive game optimization features inherited from the flagship iQOO 15 series.

Market Position & Availability

Notably, iQOO has decided to release only the standard Neo 11 model this year, without a Pro version, making it the sole focus of their mid-range performance strategy. This "all-in-one" approach positions the Neo 11 as a comprehensive package that covers all key flagship features at a more accessible price point.

Final Thoughts

The iQOO Neo 11 represents a significant leap forward for performance-focused smartphones in the mid-range segment. By combining the flagship Snapdragon 8 Elite processor with a massive 7500mAh battery and industry-leading 2K 144Hz display, iQOO has created a device that challenges not only its direct competitors but also encroaches on the territory of more expensive flagship models.

The comprehensive feature set, including the 8K VC cooling system, metal frame, IP68/69 rating, and advanced gaming optimizations, makes the Neo 11 one of the most complete packages in its class for 2025. For gamers and performance enthusiasts who want flagship-level specs without the flagship price tag, the iQOO Neo 11 sets a new benchmark that will likely influence the entire smartphone market in the coming months.

With today's launch, iQOO has firmly positioned the Neo 11 as a formidable contender in the competitive performance smartphone arena, delivering what might be the most compelling price-to-performance ratio of the year.
