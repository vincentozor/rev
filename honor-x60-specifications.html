October 26, 2025

Overview

The Honor X60 is a mid-range 5G smartphone launched in October 2024, featuring a large 120Hz display, a powerful 108MP main camera, and a substantial 5800mAh battery.

It runs on the MediaTek Dimensity 7025 Ultra chipset and operates on Android 14 with Magic OS 8.0, offering competitive performance and battery life in its segment.
