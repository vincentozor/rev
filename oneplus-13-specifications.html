OnePlus 13

October 24, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Stellar Black

Overview

The OnePlus 13 continues the brand's legacy of delivering flagship-level performance at a competitive price point, now enhanced with cutting-edge camera technology and blazing-fast charging capabilities. In partnership with Hasselblad, OnePlus has created a photography system that rivals the best in the industry while maintaining the smooth, responsive experience that users have come to expect.

Powered by the Snapdragon 8 Gen 4 processor and featuring the revolutionary 200MP main camera, the OnePlus 13 represents the perfect balance of performance, design, and value. With its signature Alert Slider, premium build quality, and optimized OxygenOS 15, this device delivers a premium smartphone experience without the premium price tag.
