Compare Xiaomi 17 Pro Max vs Google Pixel 10 Pro XL

October 27, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Titanium Black

Overview

The Xiaomi 17 Pro Max and Google Pixel 10 Pro XL present contrasting philosophies in flagship Android phones for late 2025. The Xiaomi 17 Pro Max emphasizes raw hardware power with its enormous 7500mAh silicon-carbon battery, ultra-fast 100W charging, and innovative dual-screen design featuring a 2.9-inch rear display. In contrast, the Google Pixel 10 Pro XL showcases computational photography excellence with its Pro Res Zoom system (up to 100x zoom), deep Gemini AI integration, and the custom Google Tensor G5 chip designed specifically for on-device AI processing.

Both devices sport premium 6.8-6.9 inch displays with 120Hz refresh rates, but differ significantly in their core strengths. The Xiaomi delivers exceptional battery endurance and charging speed alongside Leica-tuned optics, while the Pixel excels in computational photography, clean Android experience, and AI-powered features like Camera Coach and Magic Cue. Price-wise, the Xiaomi starts at $825 compared to the Pixel's $1,199, but the Pixel includes a full year of Google AI Pro service valued at $240. The choice ultimately depends on whether you prioritize hardware specifications and value (Xiaomi) or AI capabilities and camera intelligence (Pixel).

Final Verdict

Choose Xiaomi 17 Pro Max if:

  • Battery life is paramount with 7500mAh capacity (44% more than Pixel)
  • Ultra-fast 100W charging is essential (2.2x faster than Pixel's 45W)
  • You want maximum value at $374 less with more base storage (512GB vs 256GB)
  • The innovative 2.9-inch rear display for selfies and notifications appeals to you
  • Higher peak brightness (3500 nits vs 3300 nits) matters for outdoor use

Choose Google Pixel 10 Pro XL if:

  • You want the best computational photography with Pro Res Zoom up to 100x
  • Deep AI integration with Gemini Live, Camera Coach, and Magic Cue enhances your workflow
  • Clean Android experience with 7 years of updates (vs 3-4 years) is important
  • Qi2 Pixelsnap magnetic charging and accessories ecosystem appeal to you
  • Google ecosystem integration (Photos, Drive, Workspace) is valuable
  • Included 1 year of Google AI Pro ($240 value) and Fitbit Premium justify the premium

Bottom Line: The Xiaomi 17 Pro Max is the hardware powerhouse, delivering exceptional battery endurance, blazing-fast charging, and innovative dual-screen design at a significantly lower price. It's ideal for users who prioritize raw specifications and multi-day battery life. The Google Pixel 10 Pro XL excels in computational photography and AI capabilities, offering Pro Res Zoom technology, deep Gemini integration, and the cleanest Android experience with unmatched 7-year software support. Choose Xiaomi for hardware value and battery supremacy, or Pixel for camera intelligence, AI features, and long-term software investment.
