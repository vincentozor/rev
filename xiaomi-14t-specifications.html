Xiaomi 14T

October 24, 2025

Overview

The Xiaomi 14T is a compelling mid-range smartphone launched in September 2024, offering premium features at an accessible price point. Co-engineered with Leica, this device brings professional photography capabilities to a wider audience with its triple camera system featuring 50MP sensors and Leica Summilux optics. Powered by the MediaTek Dimensity 8300 Ultra processor built on advanced 4nm architecture, it delivers flagship-level performance with exceptional power efficiency.

With its stunning 6.67-inch AMOLED display featuring 144Hz refresh rate and 4000 nits peak brightness, IP68 water resistance, and 67W fast charging for its 5000mAh battery, the Xiaomi 14T provides an exceptional user experience. Running HyperOS based on Android 14 with Google Gemini AI integration, this device offers intelligent features including AI photography, real-time translation, and enhanced productivity tools. Starting at approximately $499-$599 USD, it represents outstanding value in the competitive mid-range smartphone market.
