Overview

The Vivo V50 is a stylish 5G smartphone designed for users who want premium features, a smooth display, reliable cameras, and long battery life at a mid-range price point.

With its sharp AMOLED screen, fast charging, capable processor, and AI-enhanced camera system, the Vivo V50 delivers an impressive balance of performance and elegance for everyday use and entertainment.
