Overview

The Motorola Razr+ 2025 is a premium foldable smartphone designed to offer a blend of modern aesthetics, flagship performance, and cutting-edge AI features. It continues Motorola’s tradition of sleek clamshell foldables with improved durability and enhanced software intelligence.

With a powerful chipset, versatile camera system, and a vibrant foldable LTPO display, the Motorola Razr+ 2025 stands as a strong rival to other luxury foldable phones in the market.
