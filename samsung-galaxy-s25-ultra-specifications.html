Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra

October 24, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro
Cosmic Black

Overview

The Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra is Samsung's ultimate flagship smartphone, announced in January 2025 and released in February 2025. It features a massive 6.9-inch display with the powerful Snapdragon 8 Elite for Galaxy processor, setting new standards for performance and AI capabilities.

With meaningful design refinements including more rounded corners, an upgraded 50MP ultra-wide camera, enhanced cooling system, and the integrated S Pen stylus, the S25 Ultra delivers a premium experience for power users and creative professionals who demand the best.

Pros & Cons

Pros

  • Snapdragon 8 Elite delivers exceptional performance (37% faster CPU, 30% better GPU)
  • Upgraded 50MP ultra-wide camera with 4x more detail capture
  • Large 6.9-inch QHD+ display with 2600 nits brightness and anti-reflective coating
  • Integrated S Pen with Bluetooth functionality for productivity
  • Premium titanium frame with Gorilla Armor 2 protection
  • 7 years of guaranteed software updates and advanced Galaxy AI features

Cons

  • Heavy at 218g, noticeably heavier than competitors
  • 45W charging slower than many competing flagships
  • Premium pricing starting at $1,299
  • 12GB RAM standard (16GB only available in limited markets)
