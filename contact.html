Frequently Asked Questions

How often do you update phone specifications?

We update our database daily with new phone releases and specification changes. Our team monitors manufacturer announcements, firmware updates, and industry news to ensure all information is current and accurate.

Can I suggest a phone to be added to your database?

Absolutely! We welcome suggestions for phones to add to our database. Please use the contact form above and select "Content Suggestion" as your subject. Include the phone model and manufacturer for faster processing.

How can I report incorrect specifications?

If you find any incorrect specifications, please contact us immediately using the form above and select "Specification Correction." Include the phone model, the incorrect specification, and the correct information with a source if possible.

Do you offer API access to your database?

Yes, we offer API access for developers and businesses. Please select "Partnership Opportunity" in the contact form, and our business development team will reach out with API documentation and pricing information.

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. Technical support and specification correction requests are prioritized and usually answered within 4-6 hours during business hours.

Can I advertise on PhoneSpec Pro?

Yes, we offer various advertising opportunities. Please select "Advertising Inquiry" in the contact form, and our advertising team will provide you with our media kit, available ad placements, and pricing information.
