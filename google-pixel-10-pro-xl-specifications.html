Overview

The Google Pixel 10 Pro XL is a premium flagship built to deliver exceptional AI performance, professional-grade photography, and a smooth user experience powered by Google’s next-generation Tensor chipset.

With a large LTPO OLED display, advanced triple-camera system, long-lasting battery life, and the latest Android software, the Pixel 10 Pro XL stands as Google’s most powerful smartphone to date.
