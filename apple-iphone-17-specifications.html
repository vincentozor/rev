Apple iPhone 17

October 26, 2025
PhoneSpec Pro

Overview

The Apple iPhone 17 is anticipated to be a major evolution in Apple's flagship smartphone line, expected to launch in late 2025. It is rumored to feature an all-new design, a significantly upgraded camera system, and a next-generation A-series chip focused heavily on on-device AI capabilities.

Expected advancements include a new ProMotion display with always-on functionality, an advanced periscope telephoto lens, and the introduction of iOS 19 with deeper system-level integration of generative AI.
