Overview

The Sony Xperia 5 VII is a premium compact flagship designed for users who want top-tier performance, a cinematic display, and professional-grade camera features in a pocket-friendly size.

With its powerful chipset, outstanding video capabilities, and signature 21:9 OLED display, the Xperia 5 VII delivers a true flagship experience without the bulk.
